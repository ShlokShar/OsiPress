June 20. Iran declares the Strait of Hormuz closed, again.

A fifth of the world's oil ships through that channel, so outlets in both countries ran it that day. Here is how each one described the event to its own readers.

Read closely. In the Iranian paper, Israel broke a ceasefire, so the strait is now closed. In the Israeli paper, the army is rounding up terrorists, and the closure is just a threat. Neither headline is lying. They start the story at different points.

The noticing you just did is the product. OsiPress does the part you can't do alone, reading coverage from both countries in their own languages with every original linked, and leaves the conclusions to you.
